# 🏥 Hospital Patient Analytics & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hospital patient data to uncover insights related to patient demographics, hospital performance, and revenue generation. The goal is to support data-driven decision-making in healthcare operations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze patient admission trends and demographics
* Identify key revenue-generating medical conditions
* Evaluate hospital performance and patient load
* Measure operational efficiency using KPIs like Length of Stay (ALOS)
* Build an interactive dashboard for business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)** → Data Cleaning & EDA
* **MySQL** → Data Storage & Advanced Queries
* **Power BI** → Dashboard & Data Visualization
* **Excel** → Summary Reporting

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle (Hospital Patient Records Dataset)
* Records: 50,000+ patients
* Features include:

  * Patient demographics (age, gender, blood type)
  * Medical condition & treatment details
  * Admission & discharge dates
  * Billing amount & insurance provider

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning (Python)

* Handled missing values and duplicates
* Standardized text fields (names, conditions, hospitals)
* Converted date columns to proper format
* Created new feature:

  * **Length of Stay (ALOS)**

---

## 🗄️ SQL Analysis

* Created structured database and tables
* Wrote **15+ SQL queries** including:

  * Aggregations (SUM, AVG, COUNT)
  * Group By analysis
  * Window functions (RANK, running totals)
  * CTEs for advanced insights

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Key insights:

* Majority of patients belong to the **30–50 age group**
* Certain medical conditions contribute significantly to revenue
* Patient admissions show monthly trends
* Length of stay indicates operational efficiency

---

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard

### Key Features:

* KPI Cards:

  * Total Revenue
  * Average Length of Stay (ALOS)
  * Total Patients
  * Average Billing

* Visualizations:

  * Revenue by Medical Condition
  * Monthly Patient Admissions Trend
  * Gender Distribution
  * Top Hospitals by Patient Count
  * Length of Stay Distribution

* Interactive Filters:

  * Gender
  * Medical Condition
  * Hospital
  * Admission Type

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* High-revenue medical conditions drive hospital profitability
* Balanced gender distribution indicates diverse patient base
* Certain hospitals handle higher patient loads
* Admission trends help in resource planning
